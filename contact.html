Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Items must be in original condition with tags attached for a full refund.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We're working on expanding our shipping options to international customers.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website or the carrier's site.
